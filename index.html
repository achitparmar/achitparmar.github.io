My Story

From Engineering Student
to Growth Systems Builder

I didn't follow a straight path. I followed a pattern — systems, logic, and structure. Here's how it all connects.

My background is in Computer Science & Engineering. I've always been drawn to how systems work — how one part connects to another to produce a result. Over time, that same thinking moved naturally into marketing, funnels, and client acquisition. I didn't just learn tactics. I learned how to engineer outcomes.

🎬
2020 — First Experiments

Live Learn Earn — Content & Digital Marketing

Started a YouTube and Instagram channel. Built an audience from zero. Learned content creation, audience psychology, and the fundamentals of digital marketing. This is where I first understood what it means to earn attention before you earn money.

🏢
Agency Era

Revibe Academy — Real Agency Experience

Worked inside a social media marketing agency. I saw how real businesses struggle with growth — the gap between what agencies promise and what they actually deliver. I learned how to build social strategy, manage clients, and find what actually moves the needle for a business.

📊
Performance Marketing Phase

Internships — Where Theory Became Execution

Worked on paid ads, tracking setups, funnel builds, and client communication. Moved from learning to doing. I understood how acquisition truly works — not from theory, but from running campaigns where every rupee had to perform.

🚀
Building Systems

Arka Consultancy — Structured Growth Systems

Co-built a one-stop growth solution for ecommerce brands. Focused on funnel architecture, paid traffic, and conversion optimization. This is where I stopped thinking in campaigns and started thinking in systems.

🎯
Today

Client Acquisition Systems — One Clear Focus

Now I focus on one thing: designing clear, simple systems that help businesses get predictable clients every single month. No guessing. No random tactics. Just a structure that works — even when you're not watching it.
